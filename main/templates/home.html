About us

An investment group concerned with communication and technology, working to transfer knowledge, develop expertise and offer consulting services. Relying on the branches deployed all over the world, namely Istanbul, Doha, Tunis and London, and its relationships and partnerships with several institutions there, Wisdar Network works to deliver outstanding services to its clients and support them to achieve their communication goals and develop their projects and investments in the MENA region.

Our story

Wisdar initially invested in translation, for it has an emblematic historical significance. It has always been associated with prosperity and largely contributed to the sustainability of human civilization despite the demise of many. We chose to name our Group "Wisdar" to combine both words, "Wisdom" and "Home" (Dar in Arabic) in reference to the well-known House of Wisdom and the way these libraries managed to transfer and make use of the knowledge produced by ancient civilizations until now. Machines have outplayed Man power, speed and intelligence. However, we believe that human wisdom will always contribute to flourishing and supporting civilization. This is our story, the lens through which we perceive our future.
